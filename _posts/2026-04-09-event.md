#### Important Information
- **When:** 📅 _22nd of June 2026_
- **Where:** in person: [P216, Frescati](google.se/maps/search/59.366386,+18.060924?entry=tts&g_ep=EgoyMDI2MDYxMC4wIPu8ASoASAFQAw%3D%3D&skid=20443503-8535-49ef-a413-3cfe832bf94b) **OR** 🌐 online (registration required) 

We're happy to announce our next official (hybrid) HAAM Meeting at [ICP2026](https://icp2026.palaeogenomics.org/) scheduled for **22 June 2026 in Stockholm (Sweden) 🇸🇪**.

***We are still in the planning phase. Stay tuned for upcoming updates!***

If you would like to present your research, we plan to give poster presenters/attendees without a talk at ICP2026 the opportunity to give a flash talk at the end of our meeting. Please send your expressions by the **14th of June**.

Register for the flash talks [here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe-2J4E-Zh7WUQnr2S-ymOpOIQnpTnfuEGvo7TNWjCqyFC5zQ/viewform).


>**Agenda (in CET)**: 
>
>- 14.00-14.30h: HAAM community updates by members of the steering committee (hybrid available)
>
>- 14.30-16.00h: Networking with ECR forming new research groups. (hybrid available)
>
>- 16.00-16.30h: Flash Talks



**Registration is now open!**

If you plan to attend our meeting online (or in person) please register [here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfuQexOEtRAHnjFL3OHBblf5xMVtdmlU3e-dBeh9jGaIm1Ftg/viewform?usp=dialog).
