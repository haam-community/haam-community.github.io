Following the first ever HAAM elections in October 2025, we are delighted to announce the new HAAM steering committee for the period 2025-2026! 

You can see the compisition of the Steering committee, as well as all its past members at the [Steering committee](/steering_committee) page.

In total, 48 community members voted in the elections.
All candidates received more than 50% voter approval, and were successfully elected into the steering committee.
