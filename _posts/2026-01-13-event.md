# 📣 We are proud to announce the third edition of HAAM-Summer School! 📣

Join us for an exploration of the latest methodological advances in the field of human ancient DNA!

What to expect: Both theoretical and practical in-depth sessions with leading experts in the field.
Suitable for students and early career researchers!

**Click here to [apply](https://forms.gle/ckqTgQxnEENJQMwA6).**

## Confirmed instructors and lectures: 

- Introduction to BASH & R: Thiseas Lamnidis (MPI-EVA, Germany) & Youssef Tawfik (University of Mainz, Germany)
- aDNA Lab Methods: Vincent Geyer (MPI-EVA, Germany)
- aDNA data processing (nf-core/eager): Selina Carlhoff (MPI-EVA, Germany)
- Imputation: Bárbara Sousa da Mota (Harvard University, USA)
- Bioethics: Sarah Abel (Center of Latin American Studies, UK)
- Genetic relatedness: Şevval Aktürk (Hacettepe University, Ankara, Turkey)
- IBD-based methods: Harald Ringbauer (MPI-EVA, Germany) & Sandra Oliveira (University of Zürich, Switzerland)
- Introduction to Population Genetics: Stephan Schiffels (MPI-Eva, Germany)
- Ancestry analysis: Jazmín Ramos Madrigal (University of Copenhagen, Denmark)
- Archaeological Interpretations: Maxime Brami (University of Mainz, Germany)
- F-stats: Leo Speidel (iTHEMS, Japan) & Leonardo Vaillini (University of Mainz, Germany)
- Admixture modelling: Ainash Childbayeva (MPI-EVA, Germany & Texas University, USA)
- Limitations of ancient DNA analysis: Torsten Günther (University of Uppsala, Sweden), Stephane Pyregne (MPI-EVA, Germany & CNRS, France)
