### Important Information
- **When:** 📅 _15th of June 2026, 15:00 CET_
- **Where:** 🌐 _Online (via Zoom)_ 🧑‍💻
- **Price:** 💰 _Free_

The next inaugural HAAM-Radio Webinar will be taking place online on the **15th of June, 2026**! This webinar will focus on advances in bioinformatic methods for paleogenomic analysis, in particular testing the limits of low coverage and imputation. 

We are delighted to announce the three invited speakers:
- **"Optimizing imputation and IBD segments retrieval in ancient genomes"**
Linda Ongaro @Smurfit Institute of Genetics, Trinity College Dublin, Dublin, Ireland

- **"How to build better reference panels for ancient DNA imputation?"**
Bárbara Sousa da Mota @Department of Human Evolutionary Biology, Harvard University Cambridge, MA, USA & Department of Genetics, Harvard Medical School, Boston, MA, USA
and Kiran H. Kumar @Department of Biostatistics & Center for Statistical Genetics, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI, USA
